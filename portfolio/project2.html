ABB-Gymnasiet is one of Sweden's most prominent upper secondary schools for young people interested in technology. With a solid foundation of knowledge in engineering, natural and social sciences, they give their students the conditions to understand the bigger picture. We helped ABB High School develop its brand through a new visual identity that takes its cue from the foundation of every technical solution - the grid pattern. Found in notebooks, 3D software and computer screens, the diamond pattern serves as a symbol of exploration, creation and creativity.
